# Remove Curse
*3rd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

At your touch, all curses affecting one creature or object end. If the object is a cursed magic item, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner's attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded.

**Classes**: [Cleric](z_compendium/classes/cleric.md), [Fighter (Eldritch Knight)](z_compendium/classes/fighter-eldritch-knight.md), [Paladin](z_compendium/classes/paladin.md), [Rogue (Arcane Trickster)](z_compendium/classes/rogue-arcane-trickster.md), [Sorcerer (Divine Soul)](z_compendium/classes/sorcerer-divine-soul-xge.md), [Warlock](z_compendium/classes/warlock.md), [Wizard](z_compendium/classes/wizard.md)
